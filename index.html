Contact: Requests about your data can be sent by email.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Information you provide

In short: I collect information needed to run Breeze Control.

This may include account identifiers (for sign-in), contact details you provide during support, and app settings you configure.

This information is collected directly from you when you sign in, submit support requests, or use app controls.

Sensitive information: I do not intentionally process sensitive personal information.

Operational HVAC data

The app and gateway process HVAC telemetry and control data (for example, zone temperatures, setpoints, valve state, boiler state, schedules, and history points) so the system can function correctly.

This data is collected automatically as part of normal app operation when your device communicates with the gateway and thermostat controllers.

Technical and security data

I may process technical diagnostics (for example, timestamps, request logs, and error events) to keep the Services reliable and secure.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

In short: Data is used to operate the app and keep it secure.

I use information to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions.
  • Display live HVAC state and history.
  • Apply schedule and control changes requested by users.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Protect service security and comply with law.

3. When And With Whom Do We Share Your Personal Information?

In short: I share data only as required to run the Services.

Data may be processed by infrastructure and software providers used for hosting, networking, and authentication. I do not sell your personal data.

Any third-party service provider with access to data is expected to provide the same or equal protection for user data as described in this policy and to process data only for authorized service functions.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

In short: Data is kept only as long as needed for operation and legal obligations.

Operational telemetry and logs are retained for troubleshooting and reliability. Retention periods may vary by data type and system settings. Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

You can request deletion of your account-related personal data by emailing breeze-support@burnette.io. I will process deletion requests subject to legal, security, and fraud-prevention obligations.

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

In short: Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used.

No system is 100% secure, but I use reasonable controls to reduce risk. Please use strong credentials and keep your devices secure.

6. Do We Collect Information From Minors?

In short: The Services are not directed to children under 18.

If you believe personal information from a child has been provided, contact me so I can remove it.

7. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

In short: You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

Depending on your location, you may have privacy rights under applicable laws. To make a request, email breeze-support@burnette.io.

Where consent is the legal basis for processing, you may revoke consent by contacting the support email above. Revocation does not affect processing performed before revocation.

8. Do We Make Updates To This Notice?

In short: Yes, this policy may be updated as needed.

The "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
